What changed

Five business changes matter most.

The latest production step is not a cosmetic update. It is a structural improvement to how teams create opportunities, add products, prepare quotes, and connect commercial activity to business results.

1

Faster opportunity creation

Opportunity creation is better aligned with how sales teams actually work, reducing friction at the start of the commercial cycle.

  • Cleaner entry into the pipeline
  • Faster qualification
  • Less time lost in corrections and setup
2

Simpler product creation

A guided workflow now allows product creation directly in the commercial flow, replacing a heavier side process and reducing manual handoffs and unnecessary coordination.

  • No more Excel-based work for the core process
  • Less unnecessary back-and-forth with CTI and Master Data
  • Less back-and-forth
  • Better user experience for sales
  • Faster preparation of offers
3

Standardized quote process

Quotes are now structured in a more consistent way, improving commercial quality and making the data more useful for management review.

  • Better consistency across teams
  • Stronger reporting foundation
  • Less ambiguity in commercial documents
4

End-to-end traceability

Management can now begin connecting pipeline intent with realized sales outcomes, making it possible to answer which opportunities generated which sales.

  • Better visibility for BU heads
  • Clearer understanding of incoming revenue
  • Stronger performance follow-up
  • More credible pipeline review
5

Better quote execution

A new quote PDF and direct sending from Salesforce reduce manual work and improve responsiveness and professionalism in customer communication.

  • Faster turnaround to customers
  • Better client interaction traceability
  • Better reportability of quote activity
  • Less work outside Salesforce
  • More consistent customer-facing output

  • No more Excel-based work for the core flow

  • Fewer manual handoffs

  • Clearer understanding of incoming revenue

Salesforce is now becoming a tool that links commercial intent with actual commercial outcome.
