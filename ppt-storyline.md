# Salesforce Progress Update

## Audience
BU heads, heads of sales, and global management

## Format
20-minute presentation including Q&A

## Core message
Salesforce is moving from fragmented sales administration to a more integrated revenue workflow, with a major Opportunity-to-Quote step now live in production.

## Slide 1: Why This Matters
### Title
From sales administration to revenue visibility

### Key message
The question is no longer whether Salesforce is changing, but whether those changes now create concrete value for the business. Since January 2026, the focus has been to reduce friction for sales teams, standardize commercial execution, and improve management visibility from opportunity to realized sales.

### Talk track
- There have been global questions about what is happening in Salesforce.
- The right answer is not a technical roadmap.
- The right answer is business progress: simpler selling motions, cleaner data, and stronger visibility.
- The latest production release is a concrete step in that direction.

## Slide 2: What Changed Since January
### Title
A major step is now live in production

### Key message
Since January, the main change has been the redesign and production delivery of the Opportunity-to-Quote process in `linxens-prod`.

### Talk track
- The focus was to improve the commercial path from opportunity creation to quote generation.
- This was not a cosmetic adjustment.
- It was a structural redesign of how sales teams create opportunities, add products, standardize quotes, and execute quote sending.
- The result is a more consistent operating model for sales teams and a stronger basis for management reporting.

## Slide 3: Business Impact Across the Process
### Title
Five business changes matter most

### Key message
The redesign improves speed, consistency, traceability, and user adoption across the core commercial process.

### Messages to show on the slide
1. Faster opportunity creation and progression
2. Simpler product creation directly in the sales flow
3. Standardized quote structure
4. End-to-end traceability from opportunity to sales
5. Better quote execution and customer responsiveness

## Slide 4: Faster Opportunity Creation
### Title
Less friction at the start of the sales cycle

### Key message
Opportunity creation is now better aligned with how sales teams actually work, reducing administrative friction at the beginning of the commercial cycle.

### Business value
- Cleaner entry into the pipeline
- Faster qualification
- Better consistency in commercial records
- Less time lost in setup and corrections

### Talk track
The gain here is speed and discipline. Sales teams can start the process more efficiently, which helps build a cleaner and more usable pipeline from the beginning.

## Slide 5: Simpler Product Creation
### Title
A guided workflow replaces a heavy side process

### Key message
Product creation can now happen directly in the commercial flow through a guided workflow, removing a significant amount of unnecessary administrative burden.

### Business value
- Less back-and-forth between teams
- Fewer manual handoffs
- Faster preparation of commercial offers
- Better user experience for sales
- No more dependence on Excel-based preparation for this part of the process
- Fewer unnecessary loops with CTI and Master Data for product creation

### Before / after
- Before: sales teams had to rely on the heavier New Product Request path, off-system work, and additional exchanges that slowed execution and added complexity.
- Now: the process is more direct, more guided, and more aligned with sales execution inside Salesforce.

## Slide 6: Quote Standardization
### Title
Standardized quotes create better management visibility

### Key message
Quotes are now structured in a more consistent way, which improves quality of execution and makes downstream performance tracking possible.

### Business value
- Better consistency across teams and offers
- Better comparability of commercial activity
- Stronger basis for reporting and review
- Less ambiguity in commercial documents

### Talk track
This matters beyond document formatting. Standardization is what makes commercial data usable at management level.

## Slide 7: Opportunity-to-Sales Traceability
### Title
Pipeline activity can now be connected to realized sales

### Key message
One of the most important business outcomes is the ability to connect an opportunity to the sales it ultimately generated, which was not realistically possible before in a reliable way.

### Business value
- Better visibility for BU heads and management
- Ability to answer which opportunities generated which sales
- Better review of pipeline quality versus actual business outcome
- Better foundation for decision-making and performance follow-up
- Clearer understanding of incoming revenue
- Better reportability of the commercial pipeline versus realized business

### Management soundbite
Salesforce is becoming a tool that links commercial intent with actual commercial outcome.

## Slide 8: Better Quote Execution
### Title
Quotes can be prepared and sent more professionally from Salesforce

### Key message
A new quote PDF and direct sending from Salesforce improve execution quality, reduce manual handling, and make customer communication more consistent.

### Business value
- Faster turnaround to customers
- More professional quote output
- Less manual work outside Salesforce
- Better continuity between offer preparation and customer communication
- Better client interaction traceability
- Better reporting on quote activity and customer follow-up

## Slide 9: What Sales Teams Gain
### Title
Concrete value for daily users

### Key message
The redesign is valuable because it simplifies day-to-day work, not because it adds more system features.

### Slide bullets
- No more Excel-based work for the core flow
- Less back-and-forth
- Less unnecessary back-and-forth with CTI and Master Data
- Fewer manual handoffs
- Better user experience
- Better data consistency
- Better client interaction traceability and reportability
- Clearer understanding of incoming revenue
- Better management reporting

### Talk track
This is an adoption point. If the process is simpler and more useful, the organization gets better usage and better data quality at the same time.

## Slide 10: What Is in the Pipe
### Title
Next focus: global adoption, optimization, and visibility

### Key message
The next phase is not a new technical wave. It is focused on making the delivered process land well in the business, driving global adoption, and extracting more value from it.

### Committed next steps
- Drive adoption of Salesforce globally so teams fully shift into the new way of working
- Roll out and stabilize adoption of the new Opportunity-to-Quote model
- Continue improving usability where it slows sales execution
- Strengthen reporting and management visibility around the standardized process
- Embed the new operating model across teams so the value is captured consistently

### Rule for discussion
Do not frame this as speculative feature expansion. Frame it as controlled value capture from what has just been delivered, with adoption as the main multiplier of business value.

## Slide 11: How Deployment Works
### Title
Controlled deployment, not experimentation

### Key message
The approach is to design and standardize first, release to production once stable, then drive adoption through rollout to the business.

### Slide bullets
- Design and standardization first
- Release to production once stable
- Rollout to teams for adoption
- Objective: reliable deployment with business value

### Talk track
This is important for management confidence. The objective is not to push fast technical change. The objective is to deploy controlled improvements that teams can actually use.

## Slide 12: Closing Message
### Title
Where this leaves Linxens now

### Key message
Salesforce is now becoming more useful as an operational sales tool and more credible as a management visibility tool.

### Closing lines
- More speed in the commercial process
- More consistency in how work is executed
- More visibility from opportunity to revenue
- Better foundation for broader management confidence in Salesforce

## Q&A Prep
### Likely question
What is the biggest change for the business?

### Suggested answer
The biggest change is that the commercial flow is becoming more integrated. Sales can move more directly from opportunity to product and quote, and management can start seeing a clearer link between pipeline activity and realized sales.

### Likely question
Why is this important now?

### Suggested answer
Because Salesforce credibility depends on practical business value. The latest release improves both execution for sales teams and visibility for management.

### Likely question
What should BU heads expect next?

### Suggested answer
Not a disruptive new wave of changes, but a controlled rollout of the new model with strong emphasis on global adoption, continued usability improvements, and better reporting visibility.
